# A-Level Maths Test Questions

A spread of Pure, Statistics, and Mechanics questions for testing the software.

## Pure

1. Differentiate y = (3x² + 2)⁵ with respect to x.

2. Find the equation of the tangent to the curve y = x³ − 4x + 1 at the point where x = 2.

3. Evaluate ∫₁⁴ (2x + 1)/√x dx.

4. Solve the equation 2 cos(2θ) + 1 = 0 for 0 ≤ θ ≤ 2π, giving exact answers.

5. Use the binomial expansion to find the first four terms of (1 − 3x)^(1/2), stating the range of values of x for which the expansion is valid.

6. The sequence u_n is defined by u₁ = 2 and u_{n+1} = 3u_n − 4. Find u₄ and determine whether the sequence converges.

7. Prove by induction that, for all positive integers n, the sum from r = 1 to n of r(r + 1) equals n(n + 1)(n + 2)/3.

## Statistics

8. A continuous random variable X has probability density function f(x) = kx(4 − x) for 0 ≤ x ≤ 4, and 0 otherwise. Find the value of k and calculate E(X).

9. The discrete random variable X follows a binomial distribution B(20, 0.3). Find P(X ≥ 8), giving your answer to 4 decimal places.

## Mechanics

10. A particle of mass 5 kg is held at rest on a rough plane inclined at 30° to the horizontal. The coefficient of friction between the particle and the plane is 0.4. Determine whether the particle will remain at rest when released, and if it slides, find its acceleration. (Take g = 9.8 m s⁻².)
